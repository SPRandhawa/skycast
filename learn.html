✦ What is SkyCast

About Randhawa SkyCast

Randhawa SkyCast is your personal weather companion — built to keep you connected to the sky no matter where you are or where you care about. Whether it's your home city, a distant relative's town, or your next travel destination, SkyCast puts live weather at your fingertips in one beautifully crafted dashboard.

🌍

Global Coverage

Weather data from over 200 countries and territories. From Ludhiana to London, SkyCast has you covered with reliable, real-time data.

👤

Personal Profiles

Your saved locations, preferences, and settings are tied to your personal profile — always synced, always accessible from any device.

⚡

Real-Time Updates

Weather data refreshes live so you always see the current temperature, wind speed, humidity, and sky conditions at a glance.

🎨

Beautifully Designed

Every pixel is crafted with care. SkyCast isn't just useful — it's a joy to look at, with a sky-inspired purple-gold aesthetic that matches the mood of the weather.

✦ Our Story

The Idea

Randhawa SkyCast was born from a simple frustration — switching between multiple apps just to check the weather in different cities for family and travel. There had to be a better way.

The Vision

A single dashboard where you add all your important locations — home, family, travel spots — and see live weather for each, saved to your profile and always ready.

Built with Love

SkyCast is a passion project by the Randhawa team, combining clean technology with a design that feels as dynamic and alive as the sky itself.

✦ How to Use SkyCast

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to get your personalised weather dashboard up and running in minutes.

1
Getting Started

Visit the SkyCast Homepage

Open your browser and head to the Randhawa SkyCast website. You'll land on the homepage where you can read about the app, see example weather cards, and understand what SkyCast offers.

🏠
You're on the Homepage. Look for the "Get Started" button in the top navigation or the "Check My Weather" button in the hero section to proceed.
2
Account Setup

Create Your Free Profile

Click Get Started to open the Sign Up page. Fill in your name, email address, and choose a secure password. Your profile is where all your saved locations and preferences will live.

💡
Use a real email address — you'll need it to verify your account and recover your password if needed.
📝
Fill in: Full Name · Email · Password. Then click "Create Account". You'll receive a confirmation email shortly.
3
Email Verification

Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email from Randhawa SkyCast. Click the confirmation link inside to activate your account. This keeps your data secure and your account protected.

📧
Can't find the email? Check your spam or junk folder. You can also request a new verification link from the login page.
4
Log In

Sign In to Your Dashboard

Once verified, head to the Login page. Enter your email and password. You'll be taken directly to your personal weather dashboard — your home base inside SkyCast.

🔐
Enter your Email + Password and click "Sign In". Tick "Remember Me" so you stay logged in on your device.
5
Add Locations

Add Your First Location

On your dashboard, you'll see an "Add Location" panel. Type in the name of a city or town, select the state/region, and optionally give it a nickname like "Home" or "Office". Hit Add This Location.

📍
Add multiple locations right away — your home city, a family member's city, your office, or a holiday destination. There's no limit!
➕
Fill in: City · State · Country · Nickname (optional) → Click "Add This Location". A new weather card will appear instantly.
6
View Weather

See Live Weather for All Your Locations

Your dashboard now shows a weather card for every location you've added. Each card displays the current temperature, weather condition (sunny, rainy, cloudy etc.), humidity, and wind speed — all updating in real time.

🌤️
Each card shows: Temperature · Condition · Humidity · Wind Speed · Feels Like. Click any card to expand and see the full 7-day forecast.
7
Manage Locations

Edit, Remove or Reorder Locations

You can manage all your saved locations from your profile settings. Remove cities you no longer need, rename nicknames, or reorder your cards so your most important locations appear first.

✏️
Drag and drop your weather cards on the dashboard to reorder them however you like — your preferred layout is automatically saved.
8
Stay Updated

Enable Alerts & Notifications

Head to your profile settings and turn on weather alerts for any of your saved locations. You'll be notified of extreme weather — storms, heatwaves, heavy rain — before they hit.

🔔
Go to Profile → Notification Settings → Toggle alerts ON for any location. Choose alert types: Storm, Rain, Heat, Wind.
