Overview

Welcome to Randhawa SkyCast ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

By creating an account or using Randhawa SkyCast, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our service.

In simple terms: We only collect what we need to run the service, we never sell your data, and you are always in control of your information.

Section 02

Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide you with a personalised weather experience. Here is exactly what we collect and why:

Account Information

  • Your full name — used to personalise your dashboard experience
  • Email address — used for account login, verification, and important service notifications
  • Password — stored in encrypted form; we never store or see your plain-text password

Weather & Location Preferences

  • City, region, and country names that you manually add to your profile
  • Nicknames you assign to your saved locations (e.g., "Home", "Office")
  • Your preferred temperature unit (Celsius or Fahrenheit)
  • Notification preferences and alert settings

Technical Data (Automatically Collected)

  • Browser type and version — for compatibility and bug fixing
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages visited and features used — for improving the service
  • Date and time of access
⚠️ We do NOT collect your precise GPS or device location. Locations on SkyCast are always entered manually by you — we never track where you physically are.
Section 03

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Randhawa SkyCast. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties. Here is specifically how your data is used:

  • To create and manage your personal SkyCast account
  • To save and display your selected locations and weather preferences on your dashboard
  • To send you account-related emails such as verification, password reset, and important service updates
  • To send weather alerts and notifications that you have opted into
  • To improve the performance, reliability, and features of the platform
  • To diagnose technical issues and fix bugs reported by users
  • To comply with legal obligations if required by law
We will never use your data for: targeted advertising, selling to data brokers, building marketing profiles, or sharing with any third party for commercial purposes.
Section 04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secure, dedicated database maintained by the Randhawa SkyCast team. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

  • Passwords are hashed using strong encryption algorithms — plain-text passwords are never stored
  • All data transmission between your browser and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/SSL
  • Our database is protected by access controls — only authorised personnel can access it
  • Regular security audits are performed to identify and address vulnerabilities
  • Data is backed up regularly to prevent loss
⚠️ While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your SkyCast account and never share it with anyone.

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data and saved locations will be permanently removed from our database within 30 days.

Section 05

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties. Period.

The only circumstances under which we may share limited data are:

  • Weather API Providers: To fetch weather data, we send the city/region names you have saved to our trusted weather data provider. No personal information (name, email) is ever shared with them.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority, we may be obligated to disclose certain information. We will notify you where legally permitted.
  • Service Infrastructure: Our hosting and database providers may technically process your data as part of delivering the service. These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements.
Bottom line: Your name, email, and personal details are never shared with advertisers, marketing platforms, or data brokers — ever.
Section 06

Cookies & Tracking

Randhawa SkyCast uses a small number of cookies to make the service work properly. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

  • Session Cookies: Keep you logged in while you use the app. These expire when you close your browser (unless you select "Remember Me").
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings such as temperature units and dashboard layout so you don't have to re-enter them each visit.
  • Security Cookies: Help protect your account from CSRF and session hijacking attacks.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party advertising or tracking scripts on SkyCast.

You can clear cookies from your browser settings at any time, though doing so may log you out and reset your preferences.

Section 07

Your Rights

You are in full control of your personal data. As a SkyCast user, you have the following rights:

👁️

Right to Access

You can request a copy of all the personal data we hold about you at any time.

✏️

Right to Correct

You can update or correct your name, email, and preferences directly from your profile settings.

🗑️

Right to Delete

You can delete your account at any time. All your data will be permanently erased within 30 days.

📦

Right to Portability

You can request an export of your saved locations and profile data in a readable format.

🔕

Right to Opt Out

You can disable weather alerts and email notifications from your profile settings at any time.

📩

Right to Complain

If you believe your data has been mishandled, you have the right to contact us or a relevant authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please visit our Contact Page and we will respond within 7 working days.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

Randhawa SkyCast is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created a SkyCast account, please contact us immediately via our Contact Page and we will delete the account and all associated data promptly.

Users aged 13–18 should have a parent or guardian review this Privacy Policy before using SkyCast.
Section 09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send an email notification to all registered users informing them of the changes
  • Display a notice on the SkyCast dashboard for 30 days after the update

Your continued use of SkyCast after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
